### TAK with foobar2000

[foo_input_tak](http://www.foobar2000.org/components/view/foo_input_tak)

- Custom Encoder
    - Encoder: `takc.exe`
    - Extension: `tak`
    - Parameters: `-e -p2 -ihs - %d`
- Bit Depth Control
    - Format is: lossless (or hybrid)
    - Highest BPS mode supported: 24
- Display Info
    - Encoder name: TAK

    - Settings: 2
